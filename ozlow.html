About Ozlow

Ozlow is an orange tabby with a bunch of other stuff. We got him from the shelter the same day we got his adopted sister Finly, and he immediately got sick with a sinus infection and 2 different stomach parasites. He now has a wheezy pur because of it, and sounds like a floppy disc writing when he gets really excited. He resembles closely a mountain lion, because he has a long face, a super long tail, and enormous paws.

Things Ozlow Does

  1. Flopping
  2. Meowing at 2am somewhere in the house, desperately
  3. Cuddles
  4. Runs away from most company
